Tall Trunk Galaxy 64

Developed for the SimpleFlips' Slide Hack Competition (2024), this ROM hack draws inspiration from Super Mario Galaxy's Tall Trunk Galaxy, hence the name Tall Trunk Galaxy 64. It was developed in around 20 days, during which I delved into the C source code of the original game and used Blender for 3D modeling.

The hack features a main hub with some hidden secrets and a main level focused on a slide, reminiscent of Tall Trunk Galaxy's iconic slide. The level includes two stars: one for completing the slide and another for collecting the 8 red coins, strategically placed to add an extra challenge for players.

Throughout the development, I applied various game design techniques I’ve learned over the years, focusing on creating engaging gameplay while maintaining a balance between challenge and fun. The project was a fantastic learning experience that pushed my skills in level design and game development.
